*Batman Begins*

"Batman begins (Batman Begins)":http://www.batmanbegins.com/ somewhere in a prison camp, with Bruce Wayne (Christian Bale) as a prisoner. Bruce is rescued by Henri Ducard (Liam Neeson), who later on becomes his mentor and teaches him sword-fighting, mind control. He finally tries to enlist him in his League of Shadows. Bruce refuses the offer and returns to Gotham City. Bruce have his bat suit, assorted weapons, batmobile and his cave with the help of Lucius Fox (Morgan Freeman) and Alfred (Michael Caine).

A tool for Flash, that is what "Flash-tools (Flash-tools)":http://www.flash-tools.com/ is, where you can find flashmail, flashforum, flashmp3player, flashmysql and yes a flashblog too. It have a good feature of letting you know at what stage its development of a particular project is using a visual indication of its stage from research, development, alpha, beta and to its release. You can join the mailing list if you wish, you may add your link and it also collects RSS feeds from some of the most prominent sites like, waxpraxis, philterdesign, samuel wan, flashguru, illogicz, actionscript, pnuts thoughts, k10k and web standards.
